Why Sada

We live in a time where everybody shares everything and it is easy to compare yourself with people who do not share your beliefs, your story or your rhythm.

Sada is for people who sometimes forget who they are and what they are working for. It invites you to collect the words, principles and reminders that keep you true to yourself and to come back to them when life feels noisy.
